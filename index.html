About Me

Hi! my name is Mariana Garman, I live in Seattle and this is my first attempt putting some HTML and CSS together. The kitty on the left is my sweet girl Xuxa, pronounced "Shoe-sha". We choose each other at a rescue center last Summer. Xuxa loves sleeping, rubbing her head against shoes and eating. Some of the ridiculous names she'll respond to include, but aren't limited to: Moochie, Poochie, Shoosh, Shoosh-Moosh, Beach-ball, Shoshanna, Susan, etc. Every morning Xuxa greets me with loud purrs and squinty eyes, her sweet and mellow demeanor make her the most lovely kitty on earth.
